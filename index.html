Reviews

Rebecca

We heard about this place from a friend, so we drove to Athens to check out out. It was really good. We decided today to make the trek back over to Athens, and we were not disappointed. My daughters love the Veggie Tacos. Hubby and I split a Bean Burrito Plate the first time, but got the Jalapeno Spinach Enchiladas today. Both were excellent. Wish we had a Shivam's closer to home!

Sarah

The best Mexican food I've eaten outside of Mexico. Flavorful food and huge portions! My boyfriend and I ordered the tortas-- I ordered the Hawaiian and he ordered the Cuban. We both liked his better. When we ordered two the waitress said they were really big, we said we really hungry, and in the end we had a full to-go box full of tortas. She came over to us at the end and asked us if we finished and pretty much said "I told you so" haha. We also had the papusas, and I ordered the cactus one. It tasted mostly of corn, but it was still good. Beware of the chips and salsa! So spicy. But it also kept me from filling up on them before my meal.

Cherpumple

Don't park in the back, or else you'll be towed! Assuming that you've parked correctly, enter and order from a mind boggling menu. The chips and salsa may burn your tongue from the spice but they're so good. The marascada was flavorful-has octopus, shrimp, crab, mussels with rice and avocado. I know were in a landlocked area, but I tried not to think about that while eating the seafood. Other things looked good but not necessarily mind blowing. Ask for corn tortillas, they seemed homemade.
